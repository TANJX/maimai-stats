# Maimai Arcade Pricing

A running comparison of maimai pricing at arcades around me. All prices in USD.

**Last updated:** 2026-04-26

---

## How to read this

- **Token cost** — what you pay per token (and any bulk-buy deals).
- **Tokens per round** — how many tokens one play of maimai costs.
- **Cost per round** — effective price for one play, calculated from the above.

## Gatcha — Flushing, NY

**Credits per round:** 8.2 (regular) · 6.6 (VIP)

**VIP membership:** $15.99 first month, $20.99 each consecutive month (~$247/year if continuous — first month is the loss-leader, renewals are the expensive part). Excluded from per-round math but factored into break-even below.

**Credit packages** (card activation $2, excluded from per-round math):

| Spend | Credits | $/credit | Cost/round (8.2 cr) | Cost/round VIP (6.6 cr) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| $11 | 28 | $0.3929 | $3.22 | $2.59 |
| $22 | 65 | $0.3385 | $2.78 | $2.23 |
| $33 | 107 | $0.3084 | $2.53 | $2.04 |
| $44 | 150 | $0.2933 | $2.41 | $1.94 |
| $55 | 192 | $0.2865 | $2.35 | $1.89 |
| $78 | 260 | $0.3000 | $2.46 | $1.98 |
| $108 | 379 | $0.2850 | **$2.34** | **$1.88** |

> Sign labels **$55 / 192 credits** as *Best Value*, but $108 / 379 actually edges it out at $0.2850 per credit vs. $0.2865. Realistically the $55 pack is the practical sweet spot.
>
> The $78 tier is a dud — slightly *worse* per credit than both the $55 below it and the $108 above it. Skip it.

**Headline numbers (using $55 "Best Value" pack):**
- Regular: **~$2.35 per round**
- VIP: **~$1.89 per round** (excluding membership cost)

**VIP break-even** (savings = $0.46/round at $55 pack vs. regular):
- First month ($15.99): need ~35 rounds to break even
- Each ongoing month ($20.99): need ~46 rounds/month to break even
- Annual continuous ($246.88): need ~537 rounds/year (~45/month avg)

**Single-charge bonus:** spend **$250+** in one charge and you get one free month of VIP — worth $20.99 at the renewal rate. Only useful if you'd be renewing VIP that month anyway.

> Gatcha's VIP renewal pricing is *much* more expensive than Round 1's flat annual fee. Only worth it for heavy rotation (10+ visits/month with multiple rounds each). For anyone less, regular pricing is the better call — the $15.99 first month can be a fun trial, but the $20.99 renewals don't pay back at casual volumes.

## The GAMEROOM — American Dream, NJ

**Credits per round:** 18 (confirmed)

**Wednesday discount:** 50% off — rounds run **9 credits** on Wednesdays. Halves every $/round figure below; the $110 combo drops to ~$1.02/round.

**Base packages:**

| Spend | Credits | $/credit | Cost/round (18 cr) |
|---|---|---|---|
| $10 | 40 | $0.2500 | $4.50 |
| $20 | 85 | $0.2353 | $4.24 |
| $35 | 175 | $0.2000 | $3.60 |
| $50 | 300 | $0.1667 | $3.00 |
| $75 | 550 | $0.1364 | $2.45 |
| $100 | 850 | $0.1176 | $2.12 |

**Supercharge add-ons** (only available when buying the paired base package — pay extra on top, get more credits):

| Pairs with | Add-on cost | Bonus credits | Combined total | Combined $/credit | Cost/round (18 cr) |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| $35 base | +$5 | +35 | $40 → 210 cr | $0.1905 | $3.43 |
| $50 base | +$7 | +55 | $57 → 355 cr | $0.1606 | $2.89 |
| $75 base | +$8 | +70 | $83 → 620 cr | $0.1339 | $2.41 |
| $100 base | +$10 | +125 | $110 → 975 cr | **$0.1128** | **$2.03** |

> The **$10 supercharge** is wildly efficient on its own — 125 credits for $10 ($0.08/cr), by far the cheapest marginal rate on the menu. The $100 + $10 combo is the clear winner.
>
> Watch out for the $5 supercharge on the $35 pack: $40 for 210 credits ($0.1905/cr) is *worse* than just buying the $50 base pack ($0.1667/cr). Skip the $5 supercharge unless you specifically need to stay under $50.

**Headline numbers:**
- $50 base pack (closest to $50 top-up): **$3.00 per round**
- $110 combo (best): **$2.03 per round**
- Wednesdays at 9 cr/round: **$1.50 / $1.02 per round** for the same two packs — cheapest non-VIP rate in the survey by a wide margin.
